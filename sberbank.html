UX Analytics/Research

Bank Intranet UX Analytics

Problem

Septmeber 2016 - Present

I was a part of the User Experience team in the biggest bank in Russia. The project at hand was designing a system for internal employee use that will unite the multiple systems and interfaces with which bank personnel currently interacts with on an everyday basis. I strive to understand the constraints and possibilities !!!! As a ux-analytic and researcher I was in charge of a number of teams. The bank adopted the agile mindset and work was conducted in sprints. All of me project teams were on different stages, therefore, I had the luxury of doing a range of exciting work. I was in charge of conducting usability research and design thinking sessions, user testing for ongoing projects and establishing communication between team members – from developers to product owners and designers.

Approach to solving – how I approach challenges

Since user experience was introduced to the company not long ago I also evangelized the need for usability experts on each team. One of the main issues I was faced with was balancing intuitiveness and learnability – since the system will be used by employees, there will be a certain educational phase. With the abundance of functionality and data packed into the new system, naturally we had to be smart about the interface. Design. Certain tradeoffs were inevitable, however, through continuous interaction with end users we made sure the system is tuned in to their needs.

Takeaways

After getting to know current situation and interacting with bank personnel being part of such an enormous project felt really rewarding. The new system would change working patterns and simplify work process.
