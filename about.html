Core Values

My versatile personal and educational background has given me unique perspectives to bring to the table when designing innovative products.
Always Keep Learning

Being a naturally curious person, I find learning new things an essential part of my life journey. For me traditional sources such as books, articles, courses stand alongside with travelling, trying out new activities and exploring the world. Learning new things inspires me and helps me become a better professional.

Embrace Cultural Differences

Having had the opportunity to live and study in several countries, I have developed a deeper understanding and empathy towards different cultures. I believe that communication is key to successful design. I truly enjoy engaging with different users, finding out more about their mental models and creating beautiful user experiences.

Understand the Mind

The way the mind works has always been fascinating to me. Getting education in such different spheres as communication and business allowed me to cross paths with interesting people and observe how they interact with technology. This helped me learn to base my design desicions on deep understanding of human psychology and dig deeper in order to deliver better work.
